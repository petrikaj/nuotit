### What Makes Each iOS App Different?

- **Nuotit** ‚Äî Focused on *how podcasts sound*. The only app with a full studio-grade audio toolkit (EQ, compressor, limiter, expander, voice isolation, spectrum analyzer) together with a clip export featuring dynamic burned-in captions and custom templates. European, privacy-first, on-device everything.
- **Apple Podcasts** ‚Äî Zero-friction if you're in the Apple ecosystem. Has transcripts and, as of iOS 26, an "Enhance Dialogue" ML feature for speech clarity. No manual audio processing tools.
- **Overcast** ‚Äî Marco Arment's long-running favourite. Smart Speed (shortens silences) and Voice Boost are signature features. It strictly enforces a privacy-first model with zero third-party analytics, ad services, or behavioral tracking code. Supports video clip export (up to 90 seconds) and local file uploads via Premium.
- **Castro** ‚Äî Unique inbox-style workflow: new episodes arrive as "inbox" items that you triage into your queue. Supports video clip export and local file sideloading via iCloud Drive. Now developed by Bluck Apps.
- **Pocket Casts** ‚Äî The safe cross-platform pick. Syncs across iOS, Android, and web. Natively supports Clip Sharing across its iOS, Android, and Web platforms. Supports native local file management, allowing users to import custom audio and video files via mobile share sheets or desktop drag-and-drop. Less audio-focused than Nuotit.
- **Snipd** ‚Äî AI-first approach. Generates chapter summaries, shareable highlights, video quote cards, and can export to Notion/Readwise. Supports local audio file uploads. Less traditional player, more "podcast research tool."
