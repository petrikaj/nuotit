**Nuotit** provides a full audio toolkit for listeners who want to actively shape their listening experience across all platforms.

## Who Should Choose Which?

### Choose **Nuotit** if you:
- Are in the Apple ecosystem and want best-in-class audio control
- Need professional audio processing (EQ, dynamics, voice isolation)
- Create clips for social media with captions and custom templates
- Want on-device transcription with complete privacy
- Value zero-tracking privacy

### Choose **Pocket Casts** if you:
- Need cross-platform sync (iOS + Android + Web)
- Want a reliable, polished, mature podcast app
- Prefer Trim Silence over manual audio tools
- Need a web player for desktop listening
