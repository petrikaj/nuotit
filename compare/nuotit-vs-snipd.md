# Nuotit vs. Snipd — Detailed Comparison

> Snipd uses AI for podcast summaries and highlights. Nuotit uses AI for audio processing and voice isolation. Two very different AI approaches.

## Overview

| | Nuotit | Snipd |
|---|---|---|
| **Price** | Free with Premium | Free with Premium |
| **Platform** | iOS, iPadOS | iOS, Android |
| **Developer** | single indie dev, European | Snipd GmbH (European) |
| **Focus** | Audio quality (AI for sound) | Knowledge capture (AI for content) |

---

## AI Features — Different Goals

**Nuotit's AI** focuses on *how podcasts sound*:
- AI Audio Assist identifies audio problems and suggests EQ/dynamics fixes
- Voice isolation uses AI to suppress noise

**Snipd's AI** focuses on *what podcasts say*:
- Auto-generated chapter summaries
- AI highlights of key moments
- Export to Notion, Readwise, Obsidian

These apps solve completely different problems — they're complementary, not competitive.

## Who Should Choose Which?

### Choose **Nuotit** if you:
- Want to fix and enhance how podcasts sound
- Need professional audio processing tools
- Create video clips for social media with dynamic captions
- Value complete on-device privacy
- Listen to local audio files without AI processing

### Choose **Snipd** if you:
- Use podcasts for research and learning
- Want AI-generated summaries and highlights
- Export highlights to note-taking apps
- Need cross-platform (iOS + Android)

### Use **both** if you:
- Want the best audio quality AND the best knowledge capture
- Research topics across many episodes
- Listen for both pleasure and professional development
